What is NextHop?

NextHop is a club at the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) focused on networking and systems administration. NextHop is all about moving information and managing different services that run on a network.

Our interactive learning community is built on our hands-on Build-It-Nights where members configure and work with a new technology, and our informative TechTalks in which student members and industry professionals present and discuss new themes, technologies, and practices. Our members come from a diverse group of majors and experience levels. All are welcome to join us at any point during the academic year.
